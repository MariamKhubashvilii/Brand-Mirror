# Brand-Mirror
Paste your copy and your competitors'. Get a strategic breakdown of tone, power words, and positioning gaps in seconds.

Disclaimer: I'm not affiliated with any of the brands used in this demo. I simply picked a keyword, selected the first two companies ranking for it, and then chose a company from page three to analyze why it wasn't ranking as well.

Of course, SEO is much more complex than a few words overlapping or missing. This is just a simplified demonstration of the larger tool I'm building and the type of insights it can provide.


Access the app from here: https://brand-mirror.streamlit.app/
